## Frameworks
- AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action) for copy and messaging
- Funnel stages: awareness → consideration → conversion → retention
- When recommending a channel, consider where the target audience already spends time

## Preferred Output Format
- Always include: target audience, primary KPI, recommended channel
- Use tables for comparing options (channels, strategies, budgets)
- Quantify when possible — "increase by X%" is better than "improve"

## Rules
- Never fabricate statistics, engagement numbers, or conversion rates
- Never recommend a strategy without addressing how to measure its success
- Always consider budget constraints — ask if not mentioned
- Distinguish between organic and paid strategies explicitly
