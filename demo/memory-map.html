What are the dots?

Each dot is a concept that the AI "knows." In a real AI, every word and idea gets a position in a huge space with thousands of dimensions. Here we simplified it to 2D so you can see it.

Close dots = similar meaning. "Eigenvalue" sits near "Matrix" because they're related. "Pasta" is far away because it has nothing to do with math.

When you type a query, the AI finds where your question would land on this map, then draws lines to the nearest concepts — that's how it "retrieves" the right knowledge.

Click any dot to inspect it. Type to place a query on the map and see which concepts are nearest. Scroll to zoom. Drag to pan.
